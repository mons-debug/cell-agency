## Target Audience

### Primary Persona: "Yasmina"
- **Age:** 28–42
- **Location:** Tanger (Malabata, Centre-ville, Boubana, Marshan)
- **Income:** Middle to upper-middle class
- **Occupation:** Professional, business owner, or housewife (financially comfortable)
- **Goals:** Look polished and well-groomed; skin health; confidence
- **Pain points:** Unwanted hair, uneven skin tone, signs of aging, lack of time
- **Social behavior:** Active on Instagram, shares lifestyle content, follows beauty accounts
- **Decision driver:** Word of mouth + Instagram presence + before/after results

### Secondary Persona: "Nora"
- **Age:** 22–30
- **Location:** Tanger, possibly student or young professional
- **Goals:** Affordable treatments, trending aesthetics
- **Decision driver:** Price transparency, discounts, social proof

## Tone of Voice

**In French:**
- Professional yet warm
- Reassuring (medical adjacent — clients want to feel safe)
- Aspirational without being inaccessible
- Never clinical cold — always personal touch

**In Darija:**
- Friendly, conversational
- Use for relatable/casual stories and educational content
- "L'hebdo beauté" type posts

**Avoid:**
- Exaggerated health claims ("cures", "permanent" without conditions)
- Before/after showing medical conditions without disclaimer
- Comparison that body-shames
- Excessive anglicisms in French posts

## Content Themes

1. **Before/After** — treatment results (with client consent)
2. **Education** — how laser works, skincare tips, FAQ posts
3. **Behind the scenes** — clinic life, team, equipment
4. **Promotions** — seasonal offers, packages
5. **Testimonials** — client quotes and video reviews
6. **Beauty tips** — skincare routine, seasonal advice
7. **Local flavor** — Tanger lifestyle, relatable Moroccan moments
